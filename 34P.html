Party Games For 3-4 People

King's Cup

Spread a deck of cards around a cup. Players draw cards and follow the rules assigned to each card.


Ace - Waterfall. (Start drinking at the same time as the person to your left. NO-ONE stops drinking until the person before them stops.)


Two - You... choose who gets to drink.


Three - Me... gon' drink.


Four - Floor. (The last person to touch the floor takes a drink.)


Five - Guys. (All the guys at the table drink.)


Six - Chicks. (All the girls at the table drink.)


Seven - Heaven. (Raise your hand above you head. The last person to do so takes a drink.)


Eight - Mate. (Choose someone to be your mate. For the rest of the game, they drink when you drink.)


Nine - Rhyme. (You say a word, then the person next to you says a word that rhymes. The one to drink is the one who can't think of a rhyme.)


Ten - Categories. (Choose a category. Go around in a circle, naming something that falls within the category. Who can't deliver, drinks.)


Jack - Never Have I Ever. (Everyone plays Never Have I Ever.)


Queen - Questions. (Ask someone a question. That person then asks someone else a question. This goes on til someone messes up, and drinks.)


King - Pour some of your drink to the center cup. The last King drinks it.


You can play until the deck is finished or until all King's have been drawn... Or when you're too drunk.

Most Likely To

One person says, “Who’s most likely to [insert scenario]?” Everyone points to the person they think fits the description. The person with the most votes drinks.

Drunk Charades

Write phrases or actions on paper slips. One person acts it out while the others guess. The actor drinks if no one guesses correctly; the guessers drink if they’re wrong.

Picolo (App)

Download the Picolo app, which gives prompts, dares, and drinking challenges for the group. Just follow the app’s instructions.

Shot Roulette

Fill shot glasses with various drinks (some alcohol, some water/juice). Place them in a circle and spin a bottle to decide who drinks which shot.

Paranoia

One person whispers a question like, "Who’s most likely to [something embarrassing]?" to the person next to them. They answer out loud, and the group guesses the question. If they want to know, they drink.
