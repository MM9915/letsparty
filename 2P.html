Party Games For 2 People

Never Have I Ever

Take turns saying "Never have I ever..." followed by something you haven’t done.

If the other person has done it, they take a drink.

Drunk Jenga

Write challenges (e.g., "Take 2 sips," "Truth or dare") on each Jenga block.

Play normally, but follow the instructions on the block you pull.

Flip, Sip, or Strip

Flip a coin. If it’s heads, the other person takes a drink.

If it’s tails, they remove an item of clothing.

Battle Shots

Set up a small grid like Battleship and use shot glasses as “ships.”

Take turns guessing each other's ship locations, and if you hit, the opponent drinks the shot.

2 Truths 1 Lie

Each person says two truths and one lie about themselves.

The other guesses the lie. If they’re wrong, they drink.

Power Hour

Set a timer for 60 minutes and take a sip of your drink every minute. Play music and switch songs every minute to keep track.
